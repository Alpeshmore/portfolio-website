Line Follower Robot Using ATmgea8

Project Details

1. Components Required

Component Quantity Description
ATmega8 Microcontroller 1 Brain of the robot
IR Sensors (Pair) 2 or more Detects the line (black/white)
Motor Driver (L293D) 1 Controls motor speed & direction
DC Motors 2 Drives the robot
Chassis 1 Structure of the robot
Battery (9V/12V) 1 Power supply
Connecting Wires - For circuit connections

2. Working Principle

IR Sensors detect the black or white line.
ATmega8 processes sensor input and decides motor movements.
Motor Driver (L293D) controls the direction of DC motors based on ATmega8 output.
The robot moves forward, left, or right to stay on the line.

3. Circuit Diagram

The main connections include:
  • IR Sensors → ATmega8 (Input)
  • ATmega8 → L293D Motor Driver (Output)
  • L293D → DC Motors (Motor Control)
  • Power Supply for both ATmega8 and motors
    • Would you like a circuit diagram image for better understanding?

    5.Working Mechanism

    Robot moves forward when both sensors detect the line.
    Robot turns left if the left sensor moves off the line.
    Robot turns right if the right sensor moves off the line.
    Robot stops when no line is detected.

    6. Applications

    ✅ Automated guided vehicles (AGVs) in industries.
    ✅ Warehouse robots for logistics and material handling.
    ✅ Maze-solving robots in robotics competitions.
    ✅ Traffic-following vehicles for smart cities.
